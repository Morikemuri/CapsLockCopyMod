# CapsLock Copy

Minecraft Forge 1.16.5 client-side mod.

**Press ** while looking at a player to instantly copy their nickname to your clipboard.

### How it works
- Listens for  key press (no screen open)
- Ray-traces toward your crosshair to find the closest player (up to 15 blocks)
- Copies their display name to the clipboard
- Shows a confirmation message in chat
